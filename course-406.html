Course-406

Terminology through translation

Course Overview

This course provides a foundational understanding of translation studies, focusing on the practical application of translating terminology. Students will explore various translation techniques, common challenges faced during the translation process, and effective strategies to overcome them. The course emphasizes building a strong theoretical and practical base for accurate and culturally appropriate translation.

Intended Learning Outcomes

On completion of the course students will be able to:

  • Understand the core principles and theories of translation.
  • Identify and analyze common problems in translation, such as lexical, grammatical, and cultural non-equivalence.
  • Learn and apply different strategies and techniques for translating specialized terminology.
  • Develop skills in researching and selecting appropriate equivalents for complex terms.
  • Critically evaluate the quality of a translation and provide constructive solutions for improvement.
